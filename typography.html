How do I purchase an item?

If you’re after only one item, simply choose the ‘Buy Now’ option on the item page. This will take you directly to Checkout.

If you want several items, use the ‘Add to Cart’ button and then choose ‘Keep Browsing’ to continue shopping or ‘Checkout’ to finalise your purchase. You can go back to your cart at any time by clicking on the shopping cart icon at the top right side of the page.

You’ll be sent a confirmation email to verify you so make sure you check your email.
What are my payment options?
To purchase your items you can use:
  1. Your bank account or credit/debit card via PayPal.
  2. Skrill (available for non US customers only)
  3. Your Visa or Mastercard.
  4. Envato credit (existing credit only, no further purchases of credits are available).
How to download an item?
Once you've purchased your items, we recommend that you download them immediately as items may be removed from Envato Market from time to time.

To download your item:

  1. Hover the mouse over your username at the top of the screen and click 'Downloads’ from the drop down menu.
  2. Click the 'Download' button next to the item.
    1. Click ‘Main File(s)’ which contains all source files.
    2. Click ‘Licence Certificate and Purchase Code’ for licence information only.
If you haven’t already confirmed your email address, please check your inbox, or resend the email via the link in your download page.
